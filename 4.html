Vitamin A

Vitamin A is crucial for vision, immune function, and skin health. It's typically found in plant sources as provitamin A carotenoids, like beta-carotene.

Food Source Amount (per 100g)
Sweet Potato 192% DV
Carrot 184% DV
Spinach 105% DV

Vitamin A also supports the immune system and can help maintain healthy skin.

Vitamin C

Vitamin C is important for the growth and repair of tissues, and it's also a powerful antioxidant. It is commonly found in fruits and vegetables.

Food Source Amount (per 100g)
Orange 89% DV
Broccoli 135% DV
Bell Pepper 190% DV

Vitamin C also aids in the absorption of iron and boosts the immune system.

Iron

Iron is necessary for the production of hemoglobin, which helps red blood cells carry oxygen throughout the body.

Food Source Amount (per 100g)
Lentils 37% DV
Tofu 15% DV
Chickpeas 29% DV

Iron absorption can be enhanced when combined with vitamin C-rich foods.
